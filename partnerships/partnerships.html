Cause contributors

The NGO has some partner companies that support this very important cause. One of them is Santander, which has an education program for children, called "Programa Escola", thus contributing to their educational development. In addition, we have Itaú, which donates books to encourage children to read and show that dreams can come true. And finally, in 2020 two more partners joined us, Rihappy (donating toys and games) and C&A supporting us with clothes.

Each of these partners is essential to help the lives of thousands of children. This makes us more fair and responsible with the development of children and adolescents.

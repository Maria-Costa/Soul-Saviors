INITIATIVES

Souls Saviours' main goal is to combat child labor, but in addition to this, the company has several projects to help and support children and teenagers. Among them, we have the winter clothes campaign, in which we collect several pieces of cold clothing for when the winter season arrives and distribute them to children, teenagers, and homeless people. Another campaign we run mainly in September is the collection of toys and games for donation in October.

Our NGO has study classes prepared by volunteers to help the children learn, and we also take actions to buy school materials that will serve as donations for students who cannot afford them.

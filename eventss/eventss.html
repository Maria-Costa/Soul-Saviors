Charity events

In 2020, the period affected by the pandemic, we held several events to collect clothes, toys, school supplies, and others.

One of the most outstanding events held by the company was in mid-December, in which we held a Christmas event so that the children could feel the energy of Christmas and so that they could get presents. At the end of the event, the children were thrilled and happy for all the care and effort of the volunteers, who succeeded in giving a new meaning to Christmas for them
